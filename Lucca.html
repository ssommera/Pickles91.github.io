Life Before Stephen became my dad

Not much is known about where I was born. All I know is someone found me on the side of the road when I was two years old. They picked me up and dropped me off at the local humane society. I was adopted by a nice couple and their children. I felt apart of the family. One day a relative of the family came to meet me. This is the first time I met Stephen. He was a nice guy. At the time I had no idea he would later adopt me. I thought this was going to be my forever home until one day the landlord found out I was living there and I had to leave.
I was moved to a different home for awhile. It was ok, but one day they didn't want me anymore. They packed me up in their car, and took me to the house where I used to live. Instead of going in the house I was handed to Stephen. At first I was scared and a bit confused, but I remembered Stephen and knew I was going to be okay.

I have been with my dad Stephen for 6 years now and he is such a good dad to me. We play, cuddle, feeds me and gives me cat nip. I have been living my best life.
